About me
Hi! My name is Clara Boothby. I'm a third year PhD student in Informatics at Indiana University.
Research Interests
My research interests cover a broad variety of topics that generally fit under the category of science communication. I'm interested in how the infrastructure of scientific research has formed and continues to change over time. Understanding the history and patterns in science can help us understand how to present scientific knowledge efficiently and can provide ways to overcome the vast amounts of disinformation being presented daily in modern internet. I am involved in the following projects.
  • Science Genome, PI: YY Ahn
  • Author Trajectories in Embedding Space
  • The Replacement Rate of the Scientific Workforce
  • Credibility of Science Information on Online platforms
  • Text Analysis of Career Editorials
  • Axolotls
